Instructions
Need someone to just recommend one movie to you without having the hassle to go through ten different streaming apps?

This recommendation system is designed to minimize the users overthinking and contemplation.

We will give you one perfect choice so that you dont have to spend hours searching for what you like.

How to use

  • Choose the genre you are in the mood for. You can also select the preferable release date and the language.
  • We will give you 10 options, among which you have to choose the movies which you have previously seen and liked. If you like none of the options presented to you, you can ask for another set of options by pressing the refresh button.
  • That's it! You will get one movie which you will definitely like based on your previously likings.
  • Don't like the movie we suggested or have already seen it? You can ask us for another recommendation by pressing the pass button.
